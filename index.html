What is good design?

I will always be the first person to admit that my graphic design and general artistic skills are subpar on the best of days, so I don't know exactly how to create good design. I'm not the best at describing why I think certain things have good design besides surface level observations such as symmetry and appropriate color choice, so I hope to learn more about the appropriate terminology through this course.

In the Future

As mentioned previously I'd like to enter the planning field for my career. I'd like to go into either government or the private sector, though I am partial to the private sector. The reason I even began to study planning was because I've always loved maps ever since I was young, so I love making maps with GIS and I enjoy subjects like history which gave me an interest in urban studies. However, I learned that I prefered action over theory, so I decided to pursue urban planning instead. I also think it would be nice to do some good for society with my career, and the planner's job is, in theory, to create a city or town that serves the community and fulfills its wants and needs. I think the work that we do in this space will be invaluable for improving my communication skills as a government or corporate worker through the use of graphic design.
